# Company Policy

## Leave Policy
- Employees get 20 days of paid leave per year.
- Leave must be applied 2 days in advance.

## Work Hours
- Core hours are 10am to 4pm.
- Flexible start between 8am and 10am.

## Expenses
- Meals up to 500 INR per day are reimbursable.
- Travel must be approved by manager before booking.
